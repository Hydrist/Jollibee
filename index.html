Jollibee Foods Corporation (JFC) operates quick service restaurants (QSRs) under various brand names, including “Jollibee,” “Chowking,” and “Red Ribbon.” JFC also owns subsidiaries like Freemont Foods Corporation and Grandworth Resources Corporation. As of 2022, there were 1,204 Jollibee stores in the Philippines and 378 international stores across different countries.
